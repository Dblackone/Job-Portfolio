## Design Description
Justice Omoyele Residence, Ado-Ekiti, Ekiti State — interior design phase of a completed duplex. Our scope: interior furnishing, kitchen setup, bedroom fitting, TV wall installations, and full functional space integration. Following our earlier involvement as construction consultant and site supervisor, we transitioned into the interior phase with a clear understanding of the structure, dimensions, and the client's expectations.

---

## Design Philosophy
A residential interior should integrate with how a family lives — not impose a concept onto it. Kitchen, bedroom, and living spaces each designed around function first: what the room must do, then what it should look like doing it.

---

## Key Features
- TV wall installation that anchors the living space without dominating it
- Kitchen setup — appliance integration, joinery coordination, and functional layout
- Bedroom fitting — furniture specification and coordination from concept to installation

---

## Process Description
Interior scope established during the construction consultancy phase — specifications informed slab heights and wall finishes before concrete was poured. Interior design drawings produced from as-built dimensions. Fabrication coordinated with suppliers. Installation supervised and signed off room by room.
