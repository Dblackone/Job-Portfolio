## Design Description
Fina Trust Microfinance Bank Headquarters, Lagos — renovation design proposal. Commissioned as Design Consultant, we conducted an existing building assessment, developed interior renovation concepts, produced architectural visualisations, and prepared a cost estimation and detailed quotation for the client. The proposal sets out a comprehensive renovation of the HQ to improve spatial efficiency, visual identity, and staff and client experience.

---

## Design Philosophy
A banking headquarters must communicate authority before a client is seated. The renovation concept prioritises spatial hierarchy — a reception that positions the institution correctly, workspaces that function at the staff level required, and public areas that reinforce the brand rather than contradict it. Every intervention justified by function first.

---

## Key Features
- Existing building assessment as the foundation: renovation concept developed from what the building is, not imposed onto it
- Interior renovation concept with architectural visualisation — the client sees the proposal before committing to construction
- Cost estimation integrated into the proposal: no gap between design ambition and project budget

---

## Process Description
Site assessment and measured survey of existing conditions. Concept development — spatial reorganisation, material palette, lighting approach. Architectural visualisation produced for client presentation. Quantification and cost estimation. Proposal package submitted for client approval.
