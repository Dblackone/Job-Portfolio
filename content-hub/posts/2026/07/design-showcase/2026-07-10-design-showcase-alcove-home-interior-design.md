## Design Description
Alcove Home — a complete interior design project for Mr. Ferami. The scope covered the full home: seating area planning, TV wall design, embedded workspace integration, kitchen design and optimisation, welcome console design, walk-in closet design, and a custom children's double-decker bed designed and fabricated to specification. No space was treated as secondary.

---

## Design Philosophy
Every surface must work. Storage integrated invisibly into the architecture of each room. Custom fabrication where standard products could not meet the brief. The welcome console establishes the tone of the home before any room is entered — a deliberate first impression.

---

## Key Features
- TV wall with embedded workspace: two functions resolved in one wall without visual compromise
- Custom walk-in closet: storage geometry designed to the client's requirements, not off-the-shelf
- Children's double-decker bed: designed and fabricated to specification — not purchased

---

## Process Description
Brief taken from the client across all rooms simultaneously. Schematic layout proposals for each space. Design development into construction and fabrication drawings. Contractor and fabricator coordination. Site installation supervision. Final styling and sign-off.
