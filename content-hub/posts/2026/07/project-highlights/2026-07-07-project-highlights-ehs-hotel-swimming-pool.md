## Project Story
EHS Hotel in Ibafo, Ogun State required a pool that would perform — not just look complete at handover. We managed the full technical scope: pool design coordination, excavation supervision, reinforcement works, waterproofing membrane installation, mechanical systems coordination, filtration and circulation commissioning, tiling, and deck finishing. Every stage required coordination between structural, mechanical, and finishing trades on a constrained site within an operational hospitality property.

---

## Challenge / Solution / Impact
**Challenge:** Waterproofing a reinforced concrete pool shell while coordinating multiple mechanical systems — filtration, circulation, inlet and outlet placement — to a hospitality standard within a constrained site.
**Solution:** A sequenced construction programme: reinforcement, concrete pour, full curing period, then waterproofing membrane application before any tile or mechanical fit-out began. Each phase signed off before the next commenced.
**Impact:** A completed hotel-grade pool — structurally sound, mechanically operational, tiled and decked to finish — handed over to the client fully commissioned.
