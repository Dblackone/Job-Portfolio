## Project Story
Ikotun Residential Development for Nu-Avenue Resources Ltd. covers the full architectural and construction scope: architectural design and drawings, construction documentation, foundation construction, structural frame development, reinforced concrete works, blockwork, and roofing preparation. We are responsible for both the design and the execution — a combined role that demands precision at every stage. The project is ongoing and under active development.

---

## Challenge / Solution / Impact
**Challenge:** Managing a combined architect-and-contractor role on a residential development — where design decisions made at drawing stage have direct cost and buildability consequences on site.
**Solution:** Integrated design and documentation process: construction documentation produced with buildability as a primary consideration, not an afterthought. Site supervision aligned directly with the architectural drawings, no translation layer.
**Impact:** A structural frame developing to specification, with architectural intent and site execution under one team's control — fewer RFIs, fewer delays, fewer cost surprises.
