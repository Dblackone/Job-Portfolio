## Project Story
The Body Shop Nigeria required two retail outlets delivered simultaneously — Ikeja City Mall and Circle Mall, Lekki. We managed the full fit-out on both: drywall installations, mural painting integration, floor tile installation, furniture integration, interior finishing, and site coordination across both active mall environments. Working within operational shopping centres imposed strict access windows, restricted working hours, and zero tolerance for disruption to adjacent tenants.

---

## Challenge / Solution / Impact
**Challenge:** Delivering two simultaneous branded fit-outs inside operational malls, where site access and working hours were constrained, while maintaining a single brand standard across both locations.
**Solution:** Phased execution with dedicated site coordination at each location. Material procurement aligned across both sites before mobilisation. Brand-standard checks embedded at every finishing stage — no stage closed without sign-off against the client's brand spec.
**Impact:** Both Body Shop retail outlets — Ikeja City Mall and Circle Mall, Lekki — delivered to brand specification, on programme, and operationally ready for trade.
