# Content Pillars & Weekly Rotation

Daily output stays varied but systematic. Seven pillars map to seven weekdays, so
the week always covers teaching, showing, and connecting — never all sell, never
all the same.

---

## The 7 pillars

| # | Pillar | Goal | Typical format | Source |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Revit Tip** | Show technical depth, earn saves/shares | Carousel / short video | Revit, Dynamo |
| 2 | **Project Spotlight** | Prove real delivery, build desire | Render + carousel | `assets/Project Pictures/*` |
| 3 | **BIM Explained** | Educate, position as the expert | Carousel / thread | BIM workflow knowledge |
| 4 | **Render Breakdown** | Showcase visualization skill | Before/after, video | Lumion/Qmotion renders |
| 5 | **Construction Insight** | Show the on-site, hands-on side | Site photo + lesson | Site engineering experience |
| 6 | **Design Presentation** | Show concept/design thinking | Concept schema + story | Concept schema projects |
| 7 | **Engage / Personal** | Be human, drive replies | Question, BTS, poll | Personal / studio |

---

## Default weekly rotation

| Day | Pillar | Why this day |
|---|---|---|
| **Monday** | Revit Tip | Start the work-week useful; high LinkedIn intent |
| **Tuesday** | Project Spotlight | Mid-week proof; strong on Instagram |
| **Wednesday** | BIM Explained | Authority anchor; great for LinkedIn + carousels |
| **Thursday** | Render Breakdown | Visual peak; Reels/TikTok reach |
| **Friday** | Construction Insight | The hands-on differentiator; ends week grounded |
| **Saturday** | Design Presentation | Slower scroll day; concept storytelling |
| **Sunday** | Engage / Personal | Lower-sell; questions, BTS, week recap, poll |

This is the *default*. **Orders** (client-driven or campaign content) override the
slot for the day — drop the order's content into that date and shift the pillar post
to the next open slot.

---

## Monthly cadence overlay

Across a 30-day month, also ensure roughly:

- **4–6 Reels/TikToks** (one most Thursdays + ad-hoc) — reach engine.
- **8–10 carousels** — saves + dwell engine (tips & explainers).
- **6–8 single-render hero posts** — desire engine (spotlights & breakdowns).
- **4 engagement posts** (Sundays) — replies + algorithm signal.
- **1–2 "offer" posts** — soft CTA: "DM for a BIM model / design / site review."
  Never more than ~1 hard sell per week; the work *is* the sell.
