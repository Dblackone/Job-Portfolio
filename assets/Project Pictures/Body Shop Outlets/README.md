# The Body Shop Outlets — Retail Fit-Out

## Project Overview
Retail interior fit-out for The Body Shop, an international beauty and cosmetics retailer entering the Nigerian market. The work covered the brand's second and third stores in Nigeria, delivering the international brand standard to local trading floors — from bare shell through to a finished, opening-ready store.

## Project Location
Ikeja City Mall, Lagos (second store) and Circle Mall, Lekki, Lagos (third store), Nigeria.

## Project Type
Commercial Retail Interior Fit-Out.

## Project Status
Completed — both outlets trading.

## My Role and Responsibilities
Site coordination and delivery of the fit-out to The Body Shop's supplied concept and international brand standards — interpreting the brand's design package, coordinating trades and subcontractors on site, and ensuring the completed stores matched the client's specification with a high-quality local finish.

## Scope of Work
- Interpretation and coordination of The Body Shop's international brand concept and standards for local execution
- Bare-shell to trading-floor fit-out: partitioning, flooring, ceilings, joinery and MEP rough-in
- Feature mural wall installation
- Counter, shelving and product-display joinery
- Storefront signage installation

## Design & Construction Workflow / Process
The design concept and brand standards were supplied directly by The Body Shop's international design team. Our role was to interpret those drawings and standards for local site conditions, coordinate the fit-out trades, and execute the works to the brand's specification — rather than originate the design from scratch.
