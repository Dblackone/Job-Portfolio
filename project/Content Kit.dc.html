Communication

BRAND VOICE SYSTEM

DOVA Futures writes like a master builder who is also a designer — engineering precision fused with architectural aspiration. The voice is confident, precise, premium. It speaks to clients as trusted partners, never as salespeople.

Voice Characteristics by Mode

Professional Voice
"BIM coordination catches on-site conflicts before a single block is laid — saving time, money, and rework."

Use for: industry insights, process explanations, capability posts

Inspirational Voice
"A new month. Another opportunity to build something that outlasts the moment. Let's make it count."

Use for: new month posts, milestone announcements, vision content

Educational Voice
"Most clients don't know the difference between architectural drawings and construction drawings. Here's why it matters for your project."

Use for: design tips, construction tips, industry insights

Promotional Voice
"The Ado Hall project: a 600m² commercial space, delivered in 14 weeks, ₦12M under original estimate. This is what coordination looks like."

Use for: service announcements, completed project showcases

Preferred vs. Prohibited Language

Use These Words
integrated · precision · craftsmanship · engineered · handover · buildability · seamless · future-ready · transparent · landmark · coordinated · sustainable
Avoid These Words
cheap · affordable · discount · quick · easy · amazing · awesome · we're the best · unbeatable

Writing Rules

  • Person: Address clients as "you / your project / your vision." DOVA is "we / our." Never third-person self-reference.
  • Casing: Display headlines in Bebas Neue ALL-CAPS. Eyebrows are short uppercase Inter labels. Body copy is sentence case.
  • Headline style: Short, declarative, often paired clauses with full stops — never exclamation marks.
  • Numbers: Always use real metrics. Always use Naira ₦ symbol for money.
  • Emoji: Never. This is a premium professional brand.
  • Punctuation: En-dash in "design–build" is part of the brand lexicon. CTAs use first-person-plural invitations ("Let's discuss…").
  • Sentence length: 1–3 lines. Benefit-led. Mechanism second.

Graphic Style Guidelines

  • Layout: Architectural minimalism. Dark green full-bleed sections alternate with cream sections. Generous negative space.
  • Grid: 8px base grid. 1280px max container. 32px side padding. 2–3 column grids for content.
  • Borders & Radii: Sharp — 0–4px. Hairline 1px framing lines at low opacity. Avoid large rounded corners.
  • Shadows: Restrained. 0 4px 24px rgba(26,26,26,.08) for cards. No decorative drop-shadow effects.
  • Icon Style: 1.5px stroke line icons on 24px grid. Lucide icon set. Never filled icons. Never emoji.
  • Photography: Warm, architectural, daylight. Real project photography. Dark gradient protection overlay for text legibility. No stock-looking imagery.
  • Blueprint Overlay: Faint 60px grid at 3–4% white opacity on dark hero graphics — a subtle engineering/drafting cue.
  • Motion: Calm cubic-bezier(0.22,0.61,0.36,1), 0.3s. Fade + 32px rise scroll-reveals. Never bouncy or playful.

Automation Standards

AI CONTENT GENERATION RULES

Any AI system generating content for DOVA Futures must treat the following rules as absolute. No exceptions. No creative license that violates these rules.

Brand Consistency Rules

RULE 01 — BRAND VOICE
All copy must be confident, precise, and premium. Never casual, never playful, never salesy. Write like a master builder who is also a designer. Use the preferred word list from Section 03. Never use prohibited words.
RULE 02 — NO EMOJI
Zero emoji in any content — captions, graphics, CTAs, or templates. This is a premium professional brand. Use Lucide-style icons in graphics. Use line breaks and punctuation for rhythm, not symbols.
RULE 03 — CASING
Headlines in Bebas Neue are ALL-CAPS always. Eyebrow kickers are UPPERCASE Inter with wide tracking. Body copy is sentence case. Never title-case body text unless it is a proper noun.
RULE 04 — CURRENCY
All monetary references use the Naira symbol ₦ — never "NGN", never "Naira" written out when a number follows. Format: ₦350M+, ₦10M, ₦5,000,000.
RULE 05 — REAL METRICS ONLY
Only use numbers that are real and verifiable. Do not invent statistics. If exact data is unavailable, use qualitative outcome language instead. Approved benchmarks: 20+ concurrent sites, ₦350M+ project value, ₦10M+ client savings.

Design Consistency Rules

RULE 06 — COLOR
Primary surface: deep forest green #1C4636. Light surface: cream #F5EFE8. Accent: clay #B85C38 at max 10% of any graphic. Never purple, blue, or pink. Never AI-generated gradient backgrounds.
RULE 07 — TYPOGRAPHY
Display text: Bebas Neue only. Body: Inter only. No substitute fonts. No decorative or script fonts. Never Comic Sans, Roboto, or system generic fallbacks in produced graphics.
RULE 08 — IMAGERY
Use only real DOVA Futures project photography. No stock photos. No AI-generated photography. Dark gradient overlay always applied to photos used as text backgrounds. Blueprint overlay (3–4% opacity grid) on dark hero sections.
RULE 09 — LOGO
DOVA Logo - W.png on all dark/green backgrounds. DOVA Logo.png on cream/light backgrounds. Never recolor, stretch, rotate, or obscure the logo. Minimum clear space: equal to the height of the "D" letterform on all sides.

Caption Formatting Rules

RULE 10 — HOOK FIRST
Every caption begins with a hook — a declarative statement, a surprising metric, or a resonant question. Never start with "We are" or "DOVA Futures is" — lead with the value, not the brand name.
RULE 11 — CTA REQUIRED
Every caption must end with a CTA drawn from the CTA Library (Section 08). Primary lead-gen CTA must appear in at least 3 posts per week across platforms. Never end a caption with a statement — always invite action.
RULE 12 — HASHTAG PLACEMENT
Instagram: hashtags go at the end of the caption, after two line breaks, or in the first comment. LinkedIn: hashtags at the very end, 5–8 only. X: 1–3 hashtags max, integrated naturally. Never hashtags in the middle of copy.

Platform Optimisation Rules

RULE 13 — PLATFORM TONE
Instagram: visual-first, aspirational, community-engaged. LinkedIn: professional authority, thought leadership, B2B signals. X: concise, opinionated, conversation-starting. WhatsApp: direct, relationship-warm, immediate CTA. Facebook: narrative, community-friendly. Threads: casual but credible.
RULE 14 — CONTENT PILLAR BALANCE
Monthly pillar distribution: Design Inspiration 25% · Project Progress 20% · Project Showcase 20% · Educational 20% · Company Updates 10% · Seasonal 5%. No single pillar should dominate more than 30% of output in any month.
RULE 15 — QUALITY STANDARD
Every output — caption, graphic brief, or schedule — must be completable from this kit alone without additional guidance. If any element cannot be produced without external input not in this kit, flag it rather than invent it. Precision over productivity. One exceptional post beats three mediocre ones.
